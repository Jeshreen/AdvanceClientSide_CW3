Nestled between Morocco's Atlas Mountains and Djebilet Hills, Palais Namaskar graces a picturesque setting in Marrakech's revered Palmeraie region. We have 41 rooms, suites, villas and palaces, each is connected by walkways that seemingly float over pools and through Moorish arches creating intimate courtyards and secluded spaces.

Since its opening in 2012, Palais Namaskar's ambition has always been to become the world's finest hotel in Africa and our excellence has already been recognised by many of the most discerning judges in the world.
